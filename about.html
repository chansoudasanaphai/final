About Move It

Making your move smooth, easy, and stress-free.

Who We Are

At Move It, we specialize in providing top-quality moving solutions for individuals, families, and businesses. With years of experience, we handle every move as if it were our own.

Our mission is simple: deliver a stress-free moving experience with professionalism and care.

Our Services

Full-Service Moving

This is the most comprehensive option. The moving company handles everything: packing, loading, transportation, unloading, and unpacking.

Partial Packing and Moving

You pack some of your items (or none), and the moving company handles the rest.

Labor-Only Services

If you have your own truck or vehicle but need help with loading or unloading, this service provides moving labor without the transportation.

Self-Pack Moving

This service allows you to pack your own belongings. The moving company provides the truck and drivers to transport your items, but you handle all the packing.

Storage Services

Many moving companies offer short- or long-term storage options. If you can’t move into your new home right away, they’ll store your items securely until you’re ready.

Long-Distance Moving

If you’re moving across state lines or even to a different part of the country, moving companies offer specialized services for long-distance moves.\

International Moving

For those moving overseas, this option handles the logistics of moving goods to another country. The company will assist with customs regulations, international shipping, and delivery.

Specialty Moving

This is for moving large, fragile, or high-value items like pianos, fine art, antiques, or heavy equipment. Specialty movers are trained to safely handle and transport these items with the right packing materials and equipment.
